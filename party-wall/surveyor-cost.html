Cost of A Party Wall Agreement

A party wall agreement is a legal contract between two or more property owners who share a common wall, boundary, or structure.

It defines the rights and responsibilities of each owner concerning construction, repairs, or modifications to the shared structure.

Such an agreement is typically required when one owner intends to undertake work that could impact the adjoining property.
