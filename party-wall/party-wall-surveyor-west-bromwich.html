Do You Need a Surveyor for Party Wall Matters?

If you're planning construction work involving a shared wall, the likelihood of needing a party wall surveyor is high. According to the Party Wall Act 1996, both property owners must agree to any proposed work before it begins. This is particularly true when alterations affect a wall that’s shared between two properties. A surveyor’s role is to ensure that all required procedures are followed and to provide expert advice throughout the process, helping to prevent disputes and protect your interests.
