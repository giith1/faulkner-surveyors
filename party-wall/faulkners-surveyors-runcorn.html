Do I Need A Party Wall Agreement In Runcorn?

Working with a professional surveyor, such as Faulkners will help you avoid any pitfalls that may result from not meeting the required regulations. Here are some important issues to bear in mind when changing a boundary wall:

Understanding Permitted Development Rights

Permitted development rights mean people do not have to seek planning permission for some changes to a property. However, care should be taken to ensure that any modifications made are law abiding and lawful according to the building codes. A chartered surveyor can help ensure the changes you propose are within your permitted development rights.

What is a Party Wall Notice?

If you have a party wall with your neighbor, then you will need to adhere to the guidelines set by the Party Wall Act. This could involve the establishment of a party wall agreement between you and your neighbour. A surveyor can facilitate the process by serving the correct notices, drafting an agreement and then monitoring that work is completed in accordance with it.

Building Regulations

If there are alterations to a boundary wall, then these will have to satisfy the building regulations. That includes whether or not the wall is structurally sound, also in compliance with fire standards and drainage/electrical system implementation. A surveyor can advise on building regulations and their work is carried out to standard.

Boundary Disputes

From bulldozing the boundary wall, disputes over property lines with neighbors may arise in some instances. A surveyor can act as a third party when disputes arise and consult with the parties involved on proper boundary resolution.

So, changing a boundary wall is not an easy task and may require you jumping through many hoops in order to get it done.
